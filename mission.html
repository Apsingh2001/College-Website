MISSION, VISION & CORE VALUES

Mission

  • To continue to endorse the Vidyalankar tradition of providing the best of services to the student community
  • To build on our position of leadership with ethics by providing world-class education to our students.
  • To train technologists capable of thinking, and working strategically and operationally, across the regional and national boundaries.
  • To achieve this vision by providing a stimulating work environment that supports personal growth, individual contribution and team work.

Values

  • To provide the best of education to the student community through innovative and effective learning methodologies, constant improvement, cultivation of practical skills and unflinching commitment to academic quality.

Core Values

    A set of core values from the guiding force at Vidyalankar.
    As an institution and as individuals we value;

  • Honesty
  • Excellence
  • Commitment
  • Integrity
  • Responsibility
  • Salubrious Attitude
